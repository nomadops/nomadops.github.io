Why nomadops?

We're a company of digital nomads traveling and working remotely from all around the world. Most frequently we can be found in Istanbul, otherwise you'll find us working from cafes in Amsterdam, Copenhagen, Barcelona, San Francisco and beyond.

What do you do?

We believe great tools are the result of operational excellence, not the other way around. Our primary focus is on helping engineering organizations design and implement DevOps pipelines which complement and enhance their development culture. nomadops are experts in Terraform, GitHub Actions, Amazon Web Services and Kubernetes.

What else do you do?

We also build micro-startups out of products we build to solve our own problems. We're currently working on profiles.dev, a GitHub App for showcasing your developer profiles and terraform.careers, a ChatGPT driven job board.
