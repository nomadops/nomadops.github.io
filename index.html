About

Why nomadops? We're digital nomads traveling and working remotely from all around the world. Most frequently we can be found in Istanbul, otherwise you'll find us working from cafes in Amsterdam, Copenhagen, Barcelona, San Francisco and beyond.

What do you do? We believe great tools are the result of operational excellence, not the other way around. Our primary focus is on helping engineering organizations design and implement DevOps pipelines which complement and enhance their development culture. nomadops are experts in Terraform, GitHub Actions, Amazon Web Services and Kubernetes.

What else do you do? We also build micro-startups out of products we build to solve our own problems. We're currently working on profiles.dev, a tool for managing your online profiles, and dashboardmarketplace.foo, a marketplace for DevOps dasboards for multiple No-code and low-code platforms.

Why a Corgi? Corgis are the best instagram dogs! nomadops was born during the Covid lockdowns, a time when instagram videos of Corgis contributed a great deal to our mental health.

Does the Corgi have a name? Not yet, but we're open to suggestions!
